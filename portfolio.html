Portfolio

Here are some examples of our recent projects. If you are looking for something similar or completely unique, please feel free to contact me for a quote.

Exterior Cladding Re-finish

Here is a shot of a re-finishing job I did in Hampshire. For this job, I had to use all natural finished, to meet the restoration guidlines for grade 1 listed buildings.

Blue and White Rustic Finish

For this job, the customer asked me to finish the exterior of the house in a worn blue style. The customer wished the house to look as though it hadn't been painted for many years.

Modern Kitchen in White

This was a job that I did for a customer in Kent. Their old country house was due an update, so we went with a modern design that had a classic feel.

Bathroom Makeover

I had the honour of performing This job on the TV show Grand Designs! The customer wanted an eco friendly bathroom, so we installed a large skylight in the ceiling and a solar panel on the roof, to power the lights and shower.

Kids Bedroom Design

Family is always important. So fo this job, I decorated my nephew's bedroom. The tree was made by me, using a jig saw, a sander and some chalk based paint.

Hotel Balconies and Doors

A hotel in Spain hired me to paint their balconies and doors in various bright colours. I'm sure you will agree, they look fantastic.
